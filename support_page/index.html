Cool Chinese Tattoo

Mobile App for both Iphone and Android devices

Calligraphic Chinese Tattoo Creator for Kanji Tattoo Lovers For each English word user queries, the application will provide more than 10 cool Chinese Calligraphic patterns for user to pick. Those Calligraphic fonts cover a span from ancient Chinese arts to morden font designs. It will help you to find the Chinese characters which matches your other tattoos or your personality. This application also gives warnings to the common words with bad meanings only in Chinese culture.
